How It Works

From Vote to Verification

1
Step 01

Secure Authentication

Citizens authenticate using verified credentials. Zero-knowledge proofs ensure voter eligibility without revealing identity.

2
Step 02

Cryptographic Voting

Each vote is encrypted and signed with a unique cryptographic key. Anonymous but mathematically verifiable.

3
Step 03

Blockchain Recording

Vote permanently written to blockchain. Once recorded, it cannot be altered, deleted, or manipulated by anyone.

4
Step 04

Independent Verification

Voters receive a receipt to independently verify their vote was counted correctly. No trust required — just math.
